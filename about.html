Inspired by simplicity

The inspiration to create Eevivo was born from the hassle of getting a battery health check. With existing suppliers, you will have to click through a lot of steps to either order special hardware, or register your vehicle in a database. None of this should be necessary. The only requirements should be to have access to that battery data, in the simplest possible form.

Vision: the easy way

Eevivo's vision is to make battery health checks as easy as possible. We want to provide a solution that is accessible to everyone, without the need for costly specialized hardware or a registration processes leaving all your data in someone else's hands. Our goal is to empower EV owners with the information they need to understand their vehicle's battery health, in the simplest way possible. This is also how our motto was born:

Battery health certification - the easy way

History

The idea of an easy EV health check was born in the winter of 2023/2024. My old car was nearly 24 years old, and it was time for a replacement. I wanted to buy an EV, but as someone with industry experience in commercial battery systems, I was very skeptical about battery longevity. Frustratingly, at that time, most dealers were clueless if you asked about the battery's health. Nevertheless, I decided to take the plunge and get an EV.

With my newly purchased used EV, I started looking into ways to check the battery health—just out of curiosity. I quickly found that there was no easy way to do this. The only options were either dealing with clunky, unofficial apps or using specialized hardware that cost more than your curiosity was worth. At that point, I realized this was a problem that needed solving. I wanted to create a solution that would make it easy for anyone to check their EV battery health, without the need for expensive hardware or complicated processes.

And so, Eevivo was born.
